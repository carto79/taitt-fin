About

Meet Jason Taitt

Lead Financial Planner & Founder

Jason Taitt is a uniquely qualified financial planner who combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of financial psychology. As an Accredited Financial Counselor (AFC®) and Certified Financial Therapist (CFT™), Jason moves beyond simple numbers to help clients bridge the gap between their financial goals and their emotional relationship with money.

Based in Tampa, Florida, Jason founded Taitt Fin with a clear mission: to simplify the complex world of finance. With credentials from Florida State University's Center for Academic & Professional Development, he brings rigorous training and real-world experience to every client relationship.

Through his popular Substack newsletter, JTaitt Writes, Jason shares actionable insights on building wealth and cultivating the mindset required for long-term financial success.
